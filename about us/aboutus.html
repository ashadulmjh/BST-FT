ABOUT US
"We are a multi-channel auto platform with coverage and presence across vehicle types and value-added services. Through these platforms, we enable new and used automobile customers, vehicle dealerships, vehicle OEMs and other businesses to buy and sell their vehicles in a simple and efficient manner. Our vision is to create an automotive digital ecosystem which connects automobile customers, OEMs, dealers, banks & insurance companies.BST-FT is an automotive portal for cars. Customers come to BST-FT to buy new or used cars or to sell their cars. They can also research our content and use our tools such as car reviews, price guides, car specifications, car images, used car inventory, car finance offers and trade-in tools. They can inquire about a new or used car and we connect them to a new or used car dealer or OEM."
